How to Play

  • Move with WASD or Arrow Keys.
  • Aim with mouse, shoot with Space or left-click.
  • Destroy asteroids: +2 points.
  • Avoid asteroids: +1 point.
  • Hit life planets to recover 1 life.
  • Hit weapon planets to refill ammo.
  • 3 consecutive asteroid collisions = Game Over.
